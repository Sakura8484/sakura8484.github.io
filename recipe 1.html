Strawberry Smoothie

Ingredients for 2 glasses

  • 2 cups of strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/4 cup dairy free milk, e.g. oat milk
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup optional banana
  • 1 teaspoon of cocoa power
  • a cup of ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Put all the ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend on high speed till mixture is smooth and cohesive
  3. pour into a glass, sprinkle some cocoa power on top and serve
